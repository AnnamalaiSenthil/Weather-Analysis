ABOUT US

The aim of this website was to provide users with some data statistics about few of the major air quality parameters. We also provide sensor data observed and come up with our inferences. We have created our own air quality indexx parameter based on the sensor readings observed. This parameter is dependent on temperature, humidity, dust in the air and presence of CO2(smoke). This is a weighted avg followed by some scaling of the readings obtained and has been followed from research papers. The team: Team 16 comprises of Tanay Gad, Kuvam Pahuja, Aisha Harris and Annamalai Senthil.
